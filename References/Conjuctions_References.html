Referance/Conjunction

Conjunction

Conjunction is used to link or combine the words like and, or, but, then and because.

Example :

  • She will buy car or van.
  • He has one son and one daughter.
  • Mother and father is always care about their children.
  • She is look nice but not care about other.

Coordinating conjunctions is used to connect items which have the same grammatical type, ex: words, phrases, clauses. The most commonly used conjunctions are and, or, but.

Connecting worlds

  • What color is perfect? Red? Or Green?
  • What are you drinking? coffee or tea?

Connecting phrases

  • the meal is very expensive but not tasteful.

Connecting clauses

  • She was infected with the covid-19 virus but now she is recovering.

Connecting sentence

  • the country is lockdown, but the citizen does not behave very well.
  • the government is getting right decision, but people does not support it to prevent from covid-19.

ConjunctSome coordinating conjunction have two parts.

Example :

  • Either……or………
  • Neither……. nor…….
  • Both……and…….

Example :

  • He should be either football player or cricket player.
  • I haven't time to play neither cricket nor football.
  • Both Kamal and Nimal will not be complete the project withing next two days.
  • you need to trust about yourself neither government nor others to protect against covid-19.

subordinating conjunctions are used to combine with the main clause to make a sentence. The most common subordinating conjunctions are: after, although, as, before, if, since, that, until, when, while, once, as soon as, whereas. When the one of clause is used these conjunctions, it becomes a subordinate of the main clause to make upon the sentence.

Example :

  • After having proper plan, we can execute it.
  • Although the player catch ball, they could not win the cricket match.
  • If you lost, don't fall, try until achieve your goal.
  • When you wake up in the morning, try to do some exercise, because it gives healthy life to you.
  • Before you left to the school, you should eat something.

When the subordinate clause comes before the main clause you need to add comma at the end of the subordinate. If main clause come first, then no need to use comma.

Example :

  • We can execute it after having proper plan.
  • Try to do some exercise when you wake up in the morning because it gives. healthy life to you.
  • You should eat something before you left to the school.
