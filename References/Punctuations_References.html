Puctuation

Punctuation is a set of marks that regulates and clarifies the meanings of different texts. there are several marks that commonly used in the English language.

in speaking, we use pauses and the pitch of the voice to make what we say clear. Punctuation plays a similar role in writing, making it easier to read.

  • Capital Letter
  • Full Stops Marks
  • Comma
  • Colons
  • Semi-Colon
  • Quotation Marks
  • Exclamation Marks
  • Question Marks

Capital Letter & Full Stops Marks

Capital letter is used at the beginning of the sentences and Full Stops Marks is used at the end of the sentence. It’s given signal to reader that the sentence is end.

  • We need to follow the right health habits. To protect against COVID-19 disease.

And also, Capital letter is used at the beginning of the Noun. As example Name of the Person, nationality, language, days of week, months of year, Public holidays, and also geographical place.

Example:

  • The Russian government announced that they had registered the first COVID-19 vaccine for animals.
  • Google was founded on September 4, 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin.

Comma

commas are used to separate list of phrase or similar words. And also remember that we normally don’t use comma before and at the end of a list of single words. In speaking the comma is pause voice slightly.

Example:

  • The Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday and Sunday are days of week.
  • World continents in order from largest in area to smallest, these seven regions are Asia, Africa, North America, South America, Antarctica, Europe, Australia.

Commas is used for separating the clauses.

When main clauses are separated by and, or, but ,we don’t normally use a comma if the clauses have the same subject. However, we sometimes use commas if the clauses have different subjects.

Example of same subject appear in same clauses:

  • Leo Messi is football player and he is number 1 player in the world.
  • William Henry Gates is owner of the Microsoft company and he is second richest in the world right now.

Example of uses of commas :

  • If you have not goal, try to make goal, and achieve it.
  • Be honest, when you are doing anything.

Colons (:) & Semi-Colon (;)

Colons is used to introduce the list.

Example :

  • There are three main thing that you need to success: Believe in Yourself, Know Your Intention, Realize You Are Already Successful.

Colons is used to indicate subtitle or subdivision of the topic.

Example :

  • Punctuation: Colons
  • Life in Provence: A Personal View

semicolons can be used instead of full stops to separate two main clauses.

Example:

  • Sri Lanka is island; Their main language is Sinhala.
  • Covid-19 is virus; It is spread globally.

Quotation Marks (‘….’ Or “…….”)

In the English the Quotation marks is used in direct speech. we include what is said within a pair of single or double quotation marks. single quotation marks are becoming more common.

Example :

  • doctors said: "always wear face masks when you move to any place to safe from the covid-19 virus. "
  • The Australian Prime Minister said: " Lockdown the country is not the better strategy for covid-19 virus."

We can place the reported speech in three different position .

Quotation mark after comma

  • The fitness trainer said, "Don't try to do too much when you at the begin."

Comma before closing quotation mark

  • "Don't try to do too much when you at the begin," the fitness trainer said.

Commas separating the reporting clause

  • "Don't try to do too much," the fitness trainer said, "when you at the begin."

Exclamation Marks (!) & Question Marks (?)

The question marks are used to make clear that what is said is a question. when use a question mark, full stop mark should not be used.

Example :

  • A: where are you from?
  • yes, I am.
  • A: are you student?
  • B: I’m from Sri Lanka.

The exclamation marks are used to indicate an exclamative clause or expression in informal writing. we used this mark to emphasize something in informal writing. and also, sometimes more than one exclamation mark can be use

Example :

  • May you be healed!
  • Listen!
  • Oh no!!! I’m in positive thinking still about the result.
